We are an Ayrshire-based orchestral flute group. The band runs on a strictly equal opportunities basis and has no affiliation to any political, religious or sectarian organisations.

Despite being an ensemble of purely flutes and percussion, our group achieves a similar level of depth and variety as can be found in a conventional orchestra. Our instrumentation includes:

  • Piccolo
  • Treble
  • Concert flute
  • Alto
  • Bass
  • Contrabass
  • Percussion

We try to provide for all tastes, and thus our repertoire includes examples of many different styles of music: e.g. classical pieces, traditional tunes, film themes, and adaptations of modern songs.

Players of any age and level of expertise are welcomed: the group currently has around 25 members ranging in ability from grade 2 to post-grade 8, and from school-age to retirees.
