Events

 

We hold several concerts annually, either to raise funds and awareness for ourselves, or as entertainment/fundraisers for local groups. We also have ensembles of various sizes available to hire for weddings, parties, and smaller events.

Each year, we compete in the Scottish Amateur Flute Association competitions, held in various locations around Scotland. More information on SAFA can be found on their website http://www.safaflute.org/.
